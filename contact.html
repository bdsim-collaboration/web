  • There also weekly meetings for BDSIM users, currently at 13:00 (UK) on Zoom. Contact Will, Stewart or Laurie if wish to join the meeting

Dr William Shields

Will is an accelerator physics Lecturer at Royal Holloway, University of London and a member of the John Adams Institute for Accelerator Science. His interest is in medical accelerators through which he has modelled proton therapy machines as well as radiobiology accelerators with LhARA, a proposed facility for which he is an accelerator physics lead. He has contributed extensively to the development of BDSIM and its utility tools. He has collaborated with several BDSIM users across a wide breadth of accelerator physics applications. He also supports users in developing their BDSIM simulations, including numerous student projects.

Dr Laurie Nevay

Laurie is a staff member at CERN

Prof. Stewart Boogert

Stewart is the director of the Cockcroft Institute for Accelerator Science and a Professor at the University of Manchester. He supports many users develop their simulations using BDSIM and he is particularly interested in coupling BDSIM to existing beam dynamics codes, for example XSuite and RFTrack. He led the development of pyg4ometry a python pacakge to quickly generate GDML geometry for use in BDSIM or other Geant4 simulations. He is a member of the Geant4 collaboration.
